MAEER has always ventured into new avenues that are important in national development. MIT Career Guidance Center (CGC) is a gateway to aspired career and helps to become competent in the world of globalization. The CGC envisages providing career services, soft skills development Programme, & preparatory courses to help students to become excellent competent and professional. In order to open new avenues of career, the institute is trying to strengthen relationship with industry, Indian and foreign universities and Alumni through more interaction/workshops/visits. It’s indeed an honor to achieve 98% placements in the best of the industry, among the eligible candidates, from the very first batch (2004-2005) passing out of the college.
